Scrambled Eggs

To make scrambled eggs, you will need the following ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add the milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Whisk the mixture until well combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter.
  5. Once the butter is melted, pour in the egg mixture.
  6. Let the eggs cook for a few seconds, then gently stir with a spatula.
  7. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are fully cooked.
  8. Remove from heat and serve immediately.

Enjoy your scrambled eggs!
